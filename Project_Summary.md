# Project Summary: NESTA's Consultancy for TCP Africa

This document provides an overview of the technical and financial proposal for the consultancy engagement with **TCP Africa**.

## Project Overview
The proposal outlines a consultancy for a **Lead Facilitator / Strategic Planning Consultant** to design and lead the **2026 Annual Staff Planning & Co-Creation Session**. The primary focus is the national expansion of the **KoraLink Program**, a digital marketplace in Rwanda connecting youth to employment and health services.

## Key Objectives
*   **Strategic Alignment:** Defining the vision and mission for KoraLink's next phase.
*   **Business Optimization:** Refining agent commission structures and partnership strategies.
*   **Operational Planning:** Creating a costed 2026 Annual Action Plan with SMART KPIs.
*   **Institutional Strengthening:** Improving internal workflows and collaboration frameworks.

## Proposed Methodology (3 Phases)
1.  **Preparation:** Stakeholder consultations, document review, and session design.
2.  **Facilitation:** A 3-day workshop covering ecosystem mapping, commission optimization, and action planning.
3.  **Documentation:** Delivery of a comprehensive session report and a fundable work plan within two weeks.

## Financial Summary
*   **Total Fee (Before Tax):** 8,760,000 RWF
*   **Total After Tax:** 7,446,000 RWF
*   **Target Value:** Approximately $1,000 USD (as per filename).
